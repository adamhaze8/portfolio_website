CNC Router

Originally, this router came with motor drivers that could support the amperage for NEMA-17 motors but not the larger NEMA-23 motors that the mechanical structure was designed for. Because of this, I decided to completely redo the onboard electronics. This involved:

  • Replacing motor drivers
  • Adding endstops
  • Adding an electrical noise filtration circuit for the endstops to prevent signal interference from the variable frequency drive
  • Adding a vacuum system to catch dust
