CO2 Concentration Mapping Mobile Robot

The objective is to create a cost-effective mobile robotic solution for Property Management firms, enhancing the efficiency of indoor CO2 concentration monitoring compared to existing market offerings.

Key Accomplishments

  • Software Stack: Devised a software stack in ROS using Python for generating CO2 concentration maps on a remotely operated mobile robot.
  • SLAM Technology: Employed SLAM technology to derive the robot's position from laser scan data, enabling correlation with CO2 measurements and subsequent interpolation for smooth data visualization.
  • Business Case: Constructed a comprehensive business case, assessed the economic life cycle, and conducted a thorough market analysis for the mobile robot.
