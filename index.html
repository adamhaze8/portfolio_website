About Me

I'm Adam, a Mechatronics and Robotics Engineering graduate from Queen's University specializing in the intersection of hardware design and advanced control software. Over the last year, my work has focused heavily on reinforcement learning, automated hardware-in-the-loop diagnostics, and precise motor control optimization for dynamic robotic systems. From designing custom cycloidal actuators to training bipedal locomotion policies, I am passionate about building robust, high-performance robots from the ground up. Welcome to my portfolio!
